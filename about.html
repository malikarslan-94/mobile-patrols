OUR STORY

Our Greatest Asset

Is Our People

Established with a commitment to both our clients and staff, alongside a strong ethos of a standards-driven approach, ZSY Services has grown to become one of the trusted National Security providers in the UK, delivering a complete range of security services to meet the demands of an ever-changing marketplace.

Our Services

Through our industry accreditations and established relationships with police, licensing offices and other associated authorities throughout the UK, we have built strong relationships with a wide range of established and prestigious clients including leading hotel brands, premium venues, and independent establishments.

We have a strong management structure offering a stringent quality of vetting and checking of all our staff, combined with our quality management systems which are regularly audited both internally and externally to adhere to British Standards and ISO 9001:2015.
