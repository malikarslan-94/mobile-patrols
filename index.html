About ZSY Services

Our Greatest Asset Is Our People

ZSY Services was established with a commitment to both our clients and staff, alongside a strong ethos of a standards-driven approach. We have grown to become one of the trusted National Security providers in the UK, delivering a complete range of security services to meet the demands of an ever-changing marketplace.

SIA Approved Contractor

Accredited under the Security Industry Authority's rigorous Approved Contractor Scheme. Every officer is SIA-licensed, vetted to BS7858, and trained to exceed industry standards.

24/7 Control Room

Our dedicated control room operates around the clock, providing real-time incident management, lone worker monitoring, and rapid response coordination across all contracted sites.
