Professional Uniformed Security for Every Environment

ZSY Security Services deploys professional, SIA-licensed security officers to commercial premises, industrial facilities, residential developments, and public buildings across the UK. Our guarding teams provide a visible deterrent, control access, monitor alarm systems, and respond to incidents with professionalism and authority.

Every guarding contract begins with a thorough site survey and risk assessment, enabling us to design a security deployment that addresses your specific vulnerabilities. Our officers receive ongoing training in customer service, first aid, fire safety, and emergency procedures, ensuring they are prepared for any situation.

  • 24/7 static guarding and gatehouse security
  • Access control and visitor management
  • Internal and external patrols
  • Incident reporting via digital platforms
