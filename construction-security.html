Construction Site Security

Building Site Protection

Round-the-Clock Protection for Building Sites

Construction sites are prime targets for theft, vandalism, and unauthorised trespass. ZSY Services provides comprehensive construction security solutions combining manned guarding, mobile patrols, and remote CCTV monitoring to protect your site, materials, and equipment throughout every phase of your project.

Our construction security officers are trained in health and safety protocols, access control procedures, and emergency response. We work alongside site managers and principal contractors to ensure security measures integrate seamlessly with your construction programme, minimising disruption while maximising protection.

  • 24/7 manned guarding and access control
  • Perimeter security and intrusion detection
  • Plant and materials theft prevention
  • Health and safety compliance support

Site Access Management

Controlled access points with visitor logging, contractor verification, and vehicle management to maintain site security and HSE compliance.

HSE Compliance Support

Security officers trained in construction health and safety requirements, supporting your compliance obligations and contributing to a safe working environment.
