Keyholding & Alarm Response

Round-the-Clock Response

24/7 Keyholding with Guaranteed Response Times

Our keyholding and alarm response service provides round-the-clock peace of mind for business owners and property managers. When an alarm activates at your premises, our trained response officers attend within a guaranteed time window, assess the situation, secure the property, and liaise with emergency services where necessary.

By entrusting your keys to ZSY Services, you eliminate the need for staff or building managers to respond to out-of-hours alarm activations. Our officers follow detailed site-specific procedures, conduct thorough internal and external checks, and provide comprehensive incident reports for every attendance.

  • Guaranteed alarm response times
  • Secure key management and audit trails
  • Liaison with police, fire, and emergency services
  • Detailed digital incident reporting

Rapid Alarm Response
