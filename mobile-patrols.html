Mobile Patrol Security Services

Visible Deterrent Patrols

Visible Deterrent Patrols with Rapid Response

ZSY Services mobile patrol officers conduct scheduled and random patrols of your premises in clearly marked security vehicles, providing a visible deterrent to criminal activity and antisocial behaviour. Mobile patrols offer a cost-effective alternative to static guarding for sites that require regular monitoring without a permanent on-site presence.

Each patrol visit includes a thorough external inspection of the perimeter, checks of all entry points, lock and alarm verification, and a digital checkpoint report submitted in real time. Our patrol vehicles are GPS-tracked, and our control room monitors every visit to ensure full accountability.

  • Scheduled and random patrol visits
  • GPS-tracked patrol vehicles
  • Real-time digital checkpoint reporting
  • Rapid response to incidents or alarms

Multi-Site Patrol Packages

Cost-effective patrol packages covering multiple sites within a defined area, with flexible scheduling to match your security requirements and budget.

Lock & Unlock Services

Scheduled lock-up and opening services for your premises, ensuring your property is secured at the end of each day and ready for business each morning.
