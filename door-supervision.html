Professional Door Supervision Services

SIA-Licensed Door Supervisors for the Late-Night Economy

ZSY Security Services is one of the UK's trusted providers of door supervisors to the late-night economy sector. We partner with prestigious venues including leading hotel brands, premium venues, and independent establishments nationwide. Our door teams deliver professional, SIA-licensed security with a commitment to both client standards and guest experience.

With a strong ethos of standards-driven service, our door supervision teams undergo stringent recruitment, screening, and vetting to BS7858 standards. Every officer wears professional ZSY-branded or client-branded uniform, and our regular management audit programme ensures consistent quality across all venues.

Our Door Supervision Offers:

  • Fully Licensed Staff
  • Professional and smart uniform
  • Regular Management Audits
  • Nationwide coverage
  • Tailor made Security solutions
  • Comprehensive PI and PL Insurance
  • Vetted to BS7858
  • 24/7 365 Control Room

Venue Safety Management

Our door supervisors manage entry queues, conduct searches where required, monitor capacity limits, and ensure responsible dispersal. All officers are trained in conflict management and responsible alcohol practices.

Licensing Compliance

Full compliance with premises licence conditions, working alongside local police and licensing officers. Our officers adhere to BS7960 standards and all SIA regulatory requirements.
